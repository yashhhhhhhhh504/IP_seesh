3 GEAR STROKE MULTIPLIER

ACTION
$
This device converts rotary motion to reciprocating motion, while multipling the input stroke
$


STUCTURE1
$
Input Gear 1 forms a revolute pair with the frame
Input Gear 1 forms a gear pair with Gear 2
Gear 2 forms a gear pair with Gear 3
Link 4 forms revolute pairs with gears 1, 2 and 3
Link 5 forms a revolute pair with the frame
Link 5 forms a revolute pair with Gear 2
Link 6 forms a revolute pair with Gear 3
Link 6 forms a revolute pair with the piston
The piston forms a prismatic pair with the frame
$



PHYPHENOMENON
$
Rotation of input Gear 1 (constant)
a. Rotation of Gear 2
b. Oscillation of Link 4
c. Rotation of Gear 3
d. Oscillation of Link 5
e. Oscillation of Link 6
f. Reciprocation of Piston
$


ORGAN
$
a. Interface between Gear 1 / Gear 2
b. Eccentricity of Link 4 on Gear 2
c. Interface between Gear 2 / Gear 3
d. Eccentricity of Link 4 on Gear 2
e. Eccentricity of Link 6 on Gear 3
f. Slot in the frame
$


INPUT
$
a. Torque to input Gear 1

c. Torque to Gear 2

SUMMARY
$
This is mainly used to circulate air. The advantage of having a foldable one is that it occupies less space when not in use.


$

ACTION

$ 
 To circulate air.

$

STATE

$
Occupies less area when in folded state.

Occupies large area when in unfolded state.
$

PHYPHENOMENON

$
Unfold the fan.

Flap the unfolded fan

$


PHYEFFECT

$
Newtonian laws of motion.

No two bodies can occupy the same space at the same time.


$


INPUT

$
Unwrapping force.

Moment.

$


ORGAN

$
Necessary that the fan is made up of a flexible material.

Necessary that the fan material possesses the necessary stiffness to circulate the air.

$



PARTS
$
Folded fan.

Unfolded fan

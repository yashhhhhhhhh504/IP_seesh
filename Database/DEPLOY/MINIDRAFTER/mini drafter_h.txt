SUMMARY
$
This is mainly used by drafters to draw.
$

ACTION

$ 
To aid the process of drawing.

To occupy less space when not in use.
$

STATE

$
Occupy less area when not in use.

Occupy large area when in use.

$

PHYPHENOMENON

$
The scales are unfolded and thus increasing the area occupied.

$


PHYEFFECT

$
Newtonian laws of motion.
$


INPUT

$
Unwrapping force.

$


ORGAN

$
Presence of a hinged joint between the links.


$



PARTS
$
Drafter.

$

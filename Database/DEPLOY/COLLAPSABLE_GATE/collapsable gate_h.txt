SUMMARY
$
This can be used to open and the close the entrance of the building.
$

ACTION

$ 
 To deploy.

To operate the entrance of the building.
$

STATE

$
The gate is open and hence it is occupying less area.

The gate is closed and hence it is occupying large area.

$

PHYPHENOMENON

$
Increase the area occupied by closing the gate.


$


PHYEFFECT

$
Newtonian laws of motion.




$


INPUT

$
Pull force.




$


ORGAN

$
Presence of a smooth sliding surface between the gate and the guide ways provided for it.






$



PARTS
$
Collapsible gate.
